BIDDAH HUSTLE

How Well Do You Know Your Brands?

I thought it would be funny to watch Brandcenter students duke it out over the one thing we all know well. BRANDS.

Overview

Biddah Hustle is a fast paced category game that pits players against each other to see who can name the most brands related to a specific item.





Download & Play for free on on any mac device today!




Game Play & Rules:















1. Players pick the player order and then choose
the color card randomizer to determine a category.













2. Start from first tile then tap the die and move that
many spaces on the board.













3. The player will swipe down to reveal item topic.













4. Players will take turns verbally listing brands related
to topic until one drops out.(Debate is encouraged)













5. The winner of that round swipes to their
corresponding player number to claim that tile.













6. Hustle Spaces: Players have 60 seconds to list
things (back and forth) related to random topic.













7. The player with the most won spaces wins the general
category and the player with the most categories wins the game.

Design Aesthetics


Icon Design:

The gestures of each hand drawn line capture the high energy the icons play within the game.

Before the App:


First Iteration:

Before fleshing out the mechanics of the game, we focused on our branding concept. We designed a physical version of the same game, using a ladder point system.

Second Iteration:

Going digital. We knew we wanted to make this game accessible to users everywhere, so they could play in any situation. The digital aspect also gives it the exciting fast pace competitive element.
