The Brandcenter Job

Video promoting our Rube Goldberg Matchbox Machiene.

The Task:

Our Business of Branding team was tasked with creating a Rube Goldberg machine for the brand, Matchbox, targeted towards 5-9 year old children. The machine could not exceed 8x8 feet and had to deliver a promotional item at the end. The first trigger of the machine had to be a person inserting a nickel and consist of at least 12 triggers.
