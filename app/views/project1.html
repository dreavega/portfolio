Virtual Reality Horror Experience

ScreamFest Horror Film Festival is an annual horror film and screenplay competition held every October in Los Angeles, CA.

Problem:

A majority of the festivals audience members come from the surrounding Hollywood area and ScreamFest is looking to expand their outreach on a national level.

Solution:

Immerse enthusiasts willing to travel for the things they love into an unforgettable sensory-shocking experience that leaves them craving for more.

Our Twist: Virtual Reality

Horror films deliver unanticipated scenarios that stimulate scary sensations. Being scared triggers the bodiesâ€™ adrenaline sensors, leaving the audience in a euphoric state. Virtual Reality presents the same unexpected blood-pumping feeling through an entirely new approach.
