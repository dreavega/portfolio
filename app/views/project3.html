e-waste:

(n.) electronic products that are no longer wanted or considered obsolete, regardless of their working condition.

The Problem

E-waste has become the Toxic Legacy of our Digital Age. Various toxic chemicals from electronic products end up in landfills throughout the world, leading to major soil, water and air contamination.


Our Analysis:

People lack the education to fix technical damages/issues themselves.

The Solution:

Give the next generation the skills to extend product life.
