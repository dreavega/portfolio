Problem: As Millennials age and postpone having children to further their education, pursue careers and travel around the world; planning to have children gets put on the back burner and before they know it they’re in their mid-30’s and early 40’s struggling to have children.

THE FACTS



Misconceptions about infertility are negatively impacting individual’s ability to conceive and become pregnant. However, this doesn’t need to be the case. New technologies available today allow family planning to be done on their own time.

THE SOLUTION



Solution: A platform that showcases fertility misconceptions, the various new assistive reproductive technologies and provides a method for personalized future family planning.



Revealing the unforeseen challenges responsible adults
may face when trying to become loving parents.











JOURNEY MARKETING EXAMPLES




Instagram Ads: Sponsored Ads would be featured on target demographic feeds and locations where physical print ads would live.




Print Ad's: Would be placed throughout busy cities and public transportation locations, like Metro Stations.

PROTOTYPE












Section One: Displays statistics focused on the user’s lifestyle choice.




Section Two: Discover information regarding any questions around infertility, new assisted reproductive technologies and common misconceptions.




Section Three: Questionnaire reveals where users stand on the fertility timeline.



Section Four: The last section provides users with the option to plan their fertility time line throughout a 5-10 year cycle. Google Calendars allows the user to receive updates and change their plan according to their future.
