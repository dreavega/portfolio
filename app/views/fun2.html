We failed at prototyping for a music hack-a-thon
and turned it into a lesson on pivoting.

"It's like a horror movie."

Overview

A line of instruments initially designed for musical experimentation, collaboration, practice, and sharing, but now accidentally for serial killers.

Challenge:

Hack Music. We most definitely hacked music.

User Testing: The sound our instrument prototype made when user tested was nightmarish, instead of beautiful...

Solution: Pivot. Providing customizable DIY background music for horror films.


The prototype:

Pegs placed in holes on all three sides pluck the spinning strings and make a uniquely horrifying sound.


The Prism Family:

Sketches of several instruments in the prism line, each inspired by a classic instrument.


PRISM: Create Something Horrific.
